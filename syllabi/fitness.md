# Fitness / Physical Education

**Instructors:** Hillary Williams & Ray Makusiewicz (Athletics Office)

## Grade Breakdown

**100% Participation-based** — 5 points daily:

| Component | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Proper Uniform | 1 |
| Participation/Behavior | 3 |
| Attendance (on time) | +1 |
| Tardy | -1 |

**Absences:**
- Excused (in Veracross): Full credit
- Unexcused: No points

## How to Get an A

This is essentially a "show up and try" class:
1. ✅ Wear proper PE uniform
2. ✅ Arrive within 5 min of bell (time to change)
3. ✅ Participate
4. ✅ Be respectful

## Dress Code (REQUIRED)

Must change into PE clothes — can't wear regular school clothes:
- Waterford navy blue athletic shorts (appropriate length)
- Waterford T-shirt (navy, white, or gray)
- Athletic/running shoes
- Sweatpants/sweatshirt if needed (Waterford branded)

⚠️ **Improper dress affects grade** (lose uniform point)
