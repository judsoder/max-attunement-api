## Grade Components

### Participation Points (10 pts/rehearsal)
Deductions for:
- Tardiness
- Forgotten music (-3 if you print and return quickly)
- No pencil
- No Yonder pouch usage
- Incomplete uniform
- Lack of attention/participation

**Absent (even EXCUSED)?**
- Must complete 15 extra practice minutes
- Submit 2-3 sentence summary of what you practiced
- Use rehearsal makeup link under "Resources"

### Weekly Practice Charts
- **90 minutes/week** (~15 min/day)
- Private lessons do NOT count
- Submit total minutes on Canvas each week
- Physical chart signed by parent at end of term

### Theory/History Homework
- Periodic assignments
- **Can redo as many times as needed** ✓
- Can count toward practice minutes (30 min max)

### Singing Tests (Weekly)
- Individual or small groups
- Live or video submission
- **Can retake as many times as needed** ✓

### Concerts/Performances (MANDATORY)
Graded on: punctuality, attire, tech rehearsal, etiquette, post-performance reflection
