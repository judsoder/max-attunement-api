## Key Policies

### Homework
- Expect ~40 min between classes
- If taking much longer, talk to teacher

### Late Work
- Missing homework: **.01 grade until submitted**
- Late nightly homework: -1 point (if satisfactory)
- Late draft for feedback: -1 point
- Late draft after final draft deadline: **NO rewrite allowed, NO feedback**
- End of term: **ZERO** for anything after 3:30pm last day of classes

### Reading Checks
- ⚠️ **Cannot be made up** unless extension was granted beforehand

### Missed Quizzes/Tests
- Make up in Testing Center
- Must complete within 2 weeks of return

### Rewrite Policy (IMPORTANT)
- Score below 80% on major take-home essay? Can rewrite up to **2 times**
- Max achievable on rewrite: **85%**
- Must complete within 2 weeks
- Must have writing conference with teacher
- ⚠️ **Cannot rewrite late work**

### Extensions
- Must request **24 hours before** due date
- Ask in person (best)
- **Student must ask** — not parents

### Academic Integrity
- **NO ChatGPT/AI** unless explicitly allowed
- No SparkNotes instead of reading
- Don't share Google Docs with classmates (unless permitted)
- If sharing with tutor/parent: share as "comment only"
- If you share and someone plagiarizes → YOU are also accountable
